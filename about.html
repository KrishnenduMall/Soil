About Us

We were established the year 2001. However, in the year 2004 the Private Limited Company was established.

This company was established with the expertise in fabrication, erection and commissioning job in the field of Air Pollution Control & ventilation System. We have received ISO9001: 2000 certification in the year 2006.

With our established reputation and growing demands, in the field of Air Pollution Control System in steel, sponge iron, cement and coal sector of India, Soil & Enviro Industries Pvt. Ltd. Was formed with increased manpower and infrastructure as a separate establishment in the year 2004 to offer prompt technical support to customers providing engineering drawings and expert technical personnel to execution of the projects all over India. The new millennium has brought challenges with different colour, taste and ideas. Entrepreneurs need to find new vistas and develop varied niches to exceed customer’s expectations which prompted us to re-dress and reinforce ourselves for having the special service basket of

  • Renovation and Modernizations – ESPs, Bag Filters & Scrubbers
  • Annual Maintenance Contract
  • Emergency shutdown / break down maintenance
